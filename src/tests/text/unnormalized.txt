Motokari nhema yakamira. Kurudyi kwayo kune murume akagumbata maoko akaisa minwe yake pamuromo. Kuruboshwe rwake kune vanhu vagara pasi vamwe vakamira. Kumusana kwake kune chitoro chakavhurwa mukova chine magasi tengi pamberi pacho pane maSolar panel maviri.
Ruva rinamashizha akawanda akasvibira egirini pamwe nemashoma ane ruvara rweyero.
Muti wemupopo wakabereka michero yakawanda. Pedyo nemuti uyu kune mimwe miti isiri yemipopo. Pasi pemuti uyu panoratidza kuti pane uswa.
Musoro wemunhukadzi wakarukwa.Wakarukwa madhiredzi erudzi rwakatsvukirira.Unorukwa uchitanga kubva kumashure zvichiuya kumberi.Zvasvika kumberi madhiredzi anoremberera achiuya kuuso.Izvi zvakarukwa zvakanaka chose.
Pamberi pezvimwe zvitoro pane mazitombo. Zvitoro izvi zvinoita sezvakavharwa uyezve zvakavakwa nezvidhinha zvikapfirirwa nemarata. Pamberi pezvitoro izvi zvakare pane marara.
Motokari zhinji dzakasiyana - siyana dziri kufamba mumugwagwa. Parutivi pane chivakwa chesimbi namarata chekugarira vanhu vakamirira motokari dzekukwira. Rumwe rutivi kune miti dzakareba dzina maruva aneruvara rwepepuru. 
Michero yakarongedzwa kuti itengeswe. Yakawanda uye yakaiswa muzvima bokisi zvine simbi mukati uyewo imwe yacho yakaiswa pamusoro pezvima kadhibhokisi. Inosanganisira mabanana pouyawo maranjisi aripo nemaapuru. Panewo zvakare zvimachipisi zvemhando dzakasiyana zviripo kuti vanhu vagotenga.
Mumugwagwa une tara, uine marara pasi vanhu vachifamba, motokari yakamira panodirwa mafuta emotokari. 
Nzvimbo inoratidza kuti izhira inofamba neveruzhinji, vaye vanofamba netsoka. Parutivi pezhira iyi pane chishendi chakagadzirwa nematanda chikavharwa nemapepa. Mukati macho mune zvinhu zvakasiyana zvoratidza kuti zvirikutengeswa.
Mumugwagwa unoita seuri pakati pedhorobha mune motokari dziri kuzvifambira dzakawanda. Nechekurutivi kwemugwagwa uyu kunewo vanhu vari kuzvifambira uye mumwe murume akagara pasi.
Makwayi ari musango arikufura akawanda uyezve mamwe ane ruvara ruchena mamwe ane ruvara rwebhurwuni rwakasangana neruchena. Pane zvimuswe zvakakora zviri kuoneka uye kuseri kwawo kune mahuswa neminzwa iriko. Nechokumberi kune gomo riri kuoneka.
Munhu arikufamba munotengeswa mbatatisi mumusika. Uye mumwe kapfeka zvichena ne nguwane yebhurawuni arikutenga mambatatisi.
Nhandare inotambirwa mitambo yakasiyana inosanganisira kiriketi kana nhabvu. Nhandare iyi ineuswa hwakawanda hupfupi hune ruvara rwegirini. 
Zvinhu zvinoiswa mumba yekutandarira zvakarongedzwa kuti zvitengeswe. Zvakanaka zvikuru uye zvakangoiswa mazitanda akati mbikiti. Pokugara ndopazoiswa utefetefe usina kunyanya kukura. Zvakarewo mhando iyi haina kunyanya kunonekwa munyika saka ndomanakiro azvakaita.
Pane gungano rakawanda pane madzimai aripo, madzimai aya akasunga madhuku mumusoro uye mamwe evadzimai aya akasunga mazambia muchiuno. vadzimai varipo vanoratidza kuti vakatarisa pasi.
Mugwagwa miviri ine tara iri  mhiri nemhiri.  Kune mumwe wacho muri kutofamba motokari. Mberi kuri kuratidza miti mikuru yemiJacaranda ine maruva akaita pepuru.  Pasi  pemiti iyi  pane sora rakaoma  rinoratidza kuti rava nenguva risina kumbowana mwando kana kunaiwa nemvura.
Mafaira eruvara rwakasiyana runoti bhuruu, girinhi nepingi. Anoshandiswa pakuchengeta mapepa akakosha anoita kuti zvinhu zvichengetedzeke.
Kune bveni rakagara pakati pematombo. Gudo iri rakagara rakabata makumbo aro. Gudo iri rinemasizo matsvuku.
Mugwagwa unofamba dzi motokari uyo une ivhu pamwechete netumweo tumatombo tudiki. Nechepadivi pomugwagwa uyu pane chi bhodhi chiripo chakanyorwao ne ruwara rutema nezvimweo zvine rúchena. Zvakare pane zvimwe zvinhu zvakadhirowewao.
Mugwagwa uri kufambawo motokari dzakasiyana siyana dzinosanganisira mabhazi nezvimwewo zvichienda kunzvimbo dzakasiyana. Mugwagwa uyu unewo mitaro ine mavara akasiyana siyana inewo zvainoratidzira pamwe chete nekureva.
Murume ari kumba muguta.Akabata bepa re yero ne bhegi kumusana.Kumberi kwake kune motokari dzakamira padivi pechivakwa.
Chikafu chekudya chakaiswa zvakanaka mundiro. Pane pamwe pane mupunga koita pamwe panenyama yenguruve, pozoitazve miriwo yakasanganaine bhinzi nemakerotsi. Chikafu ichi chakanaka uye chinovaka muviri nekuti zvizhinji zvinodiwa nemuviri munhu anozviwana paanenge achidya.
Pedyo nemumugwagwa nechekurutivi kwawo kune imba yemudurikidzanwa. Kozotiwo mumugwagwa iwowo munemotokari nhema yakamira yakabatidza mwenje.
"Mhuka ine ruvara rwebhuruu pamusoro pakaita pepfumbu. Inowanikwa mugungwa. Inonzi shumba yemugungwa kana kuti ""sea lion"". Mhuka iyi inoratidza kutyisa."
Guta guru rinedzimba dzakaturikidzana nezvitoro zvine zvitinha zvakavakwa neruvara rutsvuku, riripedo nemugwagwa unemotikari.
Wadhiropu ine ruvara rwegireyi.Ine mikova inemibato yesimbi uye iri ye bhureki yakagara pauriri rwakasvibira.Uye rumwe rupande gwune zvekuzvuva negirazi rekuzviona.
